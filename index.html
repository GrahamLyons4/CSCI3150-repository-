About Me

Hello! My name is Graham Lyons. I am a student studying web development and digital accessibility. Throughout the semester, I've learned the importance of designing experiences that work for every user, regardless of ability, device, or environment. I enjoy coding, problem-solving, and creating websites that are clean, modern, and meaningful.

My goal is to continue improving as a designer and developer by following accessibility standards, learning new tools, and building impactful projects. This class portal showcases the work I’ve completed throughout the course and reflects my commitment to universal design and professional development.
