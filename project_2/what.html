What Is Universal Design?

An approach to design that makes content usable for everyone, regardless of ability.

The 7 Principles

  • Equitable Use
  • Flexibility in Use
  • Simple and Intuitive
  • Perceptible Information
  • Tolerance for Error
  • Low Physical Effort
  • Size & Space for Approach and Use

Why It Matters

Disabilities aren’t rare — they are part of being human. Universal design means no one gets left behind. When we design for accessibility, everyone benefits: usability improves, reach expands, and content becomes future-proof.
