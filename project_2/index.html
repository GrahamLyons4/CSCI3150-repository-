Why Universal Design?

The web was built to connect people — not filter them out. Universal design ensures equal access for people of all abilities, devices, backgrounds, and generations. It’s not just ethical — it’s future-proof.

Our generation has pushed aesthetics, standards, and technology forward. The next wave is accessibility. Let's lead it.
